How to set up a patient for a blood draw

Here's the full flow, end-to-end. Plan on ~2 minutes per patient the first time, ~30 seconds once you have them on file.

  1. Add the patient to your roster. On the dashboard, scroll to Your patients and click Add patient (or Bulk add if you're loading several at once — paste a list of names + emails + phones, separated by commas or tabs). You'll need at least first name, last name, and either a phone or email so we can reach them.
  2. Click "Request labs for a patient" (top-right red button on the dashboard). A modal opens. Pick the patient you just added (or type their name). Upload the doctor's lab order PDF — our OCR reads it automatically and auto-fills patient + tests.
  3. Set the "draw by" date and check Fasting required if applicable. Add any notes for our admin team in the notes box (e.g. "Recheck A1C and lipid panel").
  4. Click Submit. The patient instantly receives an SMS + email with a secure booking link. They click it, verify their date of birth, pick a time that works for them, and we handle the rest.
  5. Track progress in real time. Back on your dashboard, expand the lab request row to see a 6-step timeline: Notified → Opened link → Booked → Specimen collected → Delivered to lab → Cycle complete. It auto-refreshes every 5 seconds — no need to call us for updates.
  6. Need to schedule a visit instead of just sending an order? Click Schedule a visit (also top-right). It opens the same flow — pick a patient, attach a lab order, set timing.
💡 Pro tip — bulk re-orders
For repeat patients (quarterly check-ins, etc.), select multiple patients in your roster with the checkboxes, then click Request labs (N) — one lab order file gets attached to all selected patients in a single click. Saves a ton of time.
