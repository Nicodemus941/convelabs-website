Common questions

Do I keep my customer relationship?

Yes. We deliver the draw under our brand and license, but the patient is yours. Visit data lives in your CRM via webhooks. The patient pays you (or us, your choice) at the rate you set within our minimums.

Is there a per-visit fee in addition to the subscription?

The subscription pays for API access (calls, webhooks, support). Visit revenue is separate — every appointment is still a ConveLabs visit at our standard rates ($87 floor to ConveLabs, remainder to the phlebotomist for visits $150+; partner-specific rates available). Your partner contract sets who collects from the patient.

What about HIPAA?

Every partner signs a Business Associate Agreement before we issue an API key. Click-through for Starter and Growth tiers. Custom-signed for Scale.

Where can you deliver visits?

Currently the greater Orlando, FL metropolitan area. Service area expands as we grow.

How fast can I integrate?

Less than a day for the REST endpoints. The Claude Code MCP connector and Zapier app drop integration to under 10 minutes. White-label booking widget is a single iframe.

Can I get exclusivity in my territory?

Yes, on the Scale tier. $500/zip/month locks the territory so no other partner can route to phlebs in those ZIPs.
