## 1. What ConveLabs provides during beta

- **Free access** to the ConveLabs API **Growth tier** for the duration of the beta period
- A dedicated Slack channel (or Teams equivalent) for direct support
- 8 hours of integration consulting (split across the beta period)
- Pre-launch access to the Claude Code MCP connector, Zapier app, and OpenAPI spec as they ship
- Per-visit revenue split at standard rates (see §5)

## 2. What Beta Partner provides

- **Honest weekly feedback** — minimum 2 hours per week of structured time (call + async)
- A **publishable case study or testimonial** if the integration succeeds (Beta Partner has approval rights on the final copy)
- A signed **Business Associate Agreement (BAA)** — separate document, must be in place before any API key is issued
- Reasonable cooperation with bug triage and reproduction requests

## 3. Beta period

- **Start:** Date this MOU is signed
- **End:** The earliest of (a) **90 days** from start, (b) Beta Partner reaches **25 booked visits via the API**, or (c) ConveLabs publicly launches the platform
- Either party may terminate with **14 days written notice**

## 4. Intent to pay (Hormozi clause)

If at the end of the beta period the API has performed to Beta Partner's satisfaction (defined as: integration is live, Beta Partner's use case is solved, no unresolved critical bugs), Beta Partner agrees to **convert to a paid plan** within 30 days of beta end.

This is non-binding — Beta Partner is not obligated to pay if the API does not solve their problem. But Beta Partner agrees in good faith that "the API works and I'm using it daily" plus "I will continue to use it" implies "I will pay."

## 5. Visit revenue (during and after beta)

API access is free during beta. Visit revenue is **separate** and applies during and after beta:

- When a patient is booked through the API, ConveLabs delivers the draw and bills per our standard rate (currently $87 business floor + remainder to the phlebotomist for visits $150 and above; partner-rate cards for lower-cost services).
- If Beta Partner wants to bill their patient and route payment to ConveLabs, we set up a B2B invoice flow with Net 15 terms.
- If Beta Partner pre-collects (e.g., it's part of a membership they already sell), they remit to ConveLabs at the per-visit rate within 5 business days of the visit.

## 6. Confidentiality

Each party will keep confidential: API documentation, pricing not yet public, technical implementation details, and any patient or staff PII observed during integration. Confidentiality survives the beta by **two years**.

## 7. HIPAA

- Both parties acknowledge that ConveLabs API processes Protected Health Information (PHI)
- A separate signed **Business Associate Agreement** is a prerequisite to any production access
- Beta Partner agrees not to access, store, or transmit PHI in any environment that is not BAA-covered

## 8. Limitations of liability

- Beta is provided "as-is" with no warranties beyond reasonable commercial effort
- Neither party is liable for indirect or consequential damages
- ConveLabs maintains $1M of professional liability insurance during beta
- Cap on direct damages: the amount of fees Beta Partner would have paid at the Growth tier ($499 × beta months)

## 9. Not a contract for services

This MOU does NOT obligate either party beyond the confidentiality and HIPAA clauses. A separate Master Services Agreement (MSA) is signed if Beta Partner converts to a paid plan.
